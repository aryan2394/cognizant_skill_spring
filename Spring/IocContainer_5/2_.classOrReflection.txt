Tumhare screenshot mein ye line hai:

Isliye likhte hain

AppConfig.class

Spring us class ko read karega.

Jaise:

@Configuration
@ComponentScan("in.coderarmy")

}

AppConfig.class

↓

Create Beans

Agar .class na likhein?
