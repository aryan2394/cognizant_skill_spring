Interview Ready Answer

Agar interviewer bole:

Difference between IoC and Dependency Injection?

Tum confidently bol sakte ho:

IoC (Inversion of Control) is a design principle where the control of creating and managing objects is transferred from the developer to the Spring Container. 
Dependency Injection (DI) is the technique used by Spring to achieve IoC by providing the required dependencies to a class through constructor, setter, or field injection.
